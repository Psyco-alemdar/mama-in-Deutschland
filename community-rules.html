Community Rules & Safety

Before using Share your experience and Marketplace, please read the basic rules. They protect mothers, babies and trust inside the platform.

This is a safety and community guideline page, not a legal document. Later, you still need Impressum, Datenschutz and Terms for a public launch in Germany.

Share your experience

1

Respect first

No insults, humiliation, racism, hate, threats, pressure or judgment against mothers.

2

No sensitive data

Do not post phone numbers, exact addresses, documents, hospital files, patient data or private photos.

3

No medical replacement

Experiences can help emotionally, but they do not replace a doctor, midwife or emergency service.

Marketplace Safety

1

Check products carefully

Always check condition, missing parts, hygiene, age, recalls and instructions before using baby products.

2

Special caution

Be especially careful with car seats, baby beds, mattresses, breast pumps, electric devices and baby carriers.

3

Meet safely

Do not publish your exact address. Meet in safe public places and avoid suspicious payment requests.

Reporting & moderation

Report bad content

Use the report button for spam, scams, unsafe products, offensive content or dangerous medical misinformation.

Moderation later

The next technical step is an admin moderation page so the platform owner can review reports and hide unsafe content.

Before public launch

Impressum

A public German website normally needs a visible Impressum with responsible operator details.

Datenschutz

You need a privacy policy explaining what data is stored, why, where and how users can request deletion.

Terms

You need terms for community posts, marketplace listings, reports, prohibited content and user responsibility.
